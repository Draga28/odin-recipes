Pasta Italiana

Description

Pasta Italiana is a simple yet deeply satisfying dish rooted in Italian tradition. Al dente pasta is tossed in a vibrant tomato sauce made from ripe tomatoes, garlic, and fresh basil, finished with a generous drizzle of olive oil.

This recipe celebrates quality ingredients kept simple. It comes together in under 30 minutes and is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner that still feels like a proper Italian meal.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti or penne
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan, to serve

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes. Add oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Simmer the sauce on low heat for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Tear in the fresh basil and stir through.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until well coated.
  8. Serve with grated Parmesan on top.

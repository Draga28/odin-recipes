Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese. Baked until golden and bubbling, it's hearty, satisfying, and perfect for feeding a crowd.

This recipe combines Italian sausage and ground beef for a deeply flavorful sauce, balanced by the creaminess of ricotta and the saltiness of Parmesan. It's a dish that tastes even better the next day, making it ideal for meal prep or leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 8 lasagna noodles
  • 500g Italian sausage
  • 750g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 450g ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat. Add sausage, ground beef, and onion. Cook 8-10 minutes until meat is no longer pink, breaking it into crumbles. Add garlic and cook 1 more minute. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer on low heat for 15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta, eggs, parsley, salt, and pepper until combined.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 noodles over the sauce. Spread half the ricotta mixture, then half the remaining meat sauce, then half the mozzarella.
  8. Repeat the layers: noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella.
  9. Top with the remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake a further 20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  11. Let rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
